Chess Piece Summary

  • Pawn: Can move forwards once unless its at the start which they can move twice and to capture they must move diagonally
  • Rook: can move as many spaces horizontally or vertical unless there is a piece in its way
  • knight: The best description for the way the Knight moves is in an L pattern, The knight is able to move two spaces froward and one space sideways
  • Bishop: can move as many spaces diagonally unless there is a piece in its way
  • Queen: Can move as many spaces diagonally or Horizontally unless there is a piece in her way
  • King: can move only one space in any direction unless there is a piece in its way
