Chess History: Chess has been around since the beginning of the 7th century which was different than modern day chess because it wasn't until the 13th century when the rules of the game were starting to be modified. It would take chess until the late 1800s when it was considered a sport and in 1886 Johannes Zukertort was pronounced the first official chess world champion
